Lets Play!

Rules of the game:

  • You will be given a random number to start the game.
  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score using the random numbers generated by each crystal. You lose the game if you total score goes above the generated number.
  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time the game starts, the number needed to win and the value of each crystal will randomly generate.
